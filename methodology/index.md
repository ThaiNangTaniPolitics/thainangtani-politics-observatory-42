# Methodology

This observatory follows a structured, transparent approach to analyzing media narratives and political communication in Thailand.

## 1. Source Selection
We monitor a range of Thai media outlets, including mainstream newspapers, online portals, and official government communication channels.

## 2. Narrative Identification
We track recurring themes, linguistic framing, editorial choices, and shifts in tone or emphasis.

## 3. Chronological Documentation
Events, statements, and media reactions are logged in a timeline to show how narratives evolve over time.

## 4. Analytical Notes
Each entry includes observations on framing, omissions, angles, and potential strategic communication patterns.
